About me

Dr. Zhou is an associate professor (Zijiang Youth Scholar) and Ph.D. supervisor at School of Computer Science and Technology at East China Normal University. He obtained his Ph.D. in 2021 from the School of Computer Science at East China Normal University and completed his postdoctoral research from 2021 to 2023 at Fudan NLP, the director is Prof. Xuanjing Huang. He has published over 70 papers in prestigious international conferences and journals, such as AAAI, ACL, and ACM Computing Surveys, with more than 1,400 citations on Google Scholar. Dr. Zhou received the Outstanding Paper Award at COLING 2022 (as the first author), a cover paper in FITEE, and the Shanghai Science and Technology Progress Award (Second Prize, third contributor).
