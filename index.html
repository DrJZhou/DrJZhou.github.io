About me

Dr. Zhou is an associate professor in School of Computer Science and Technology at East China Normal University. He was also very fortunate to work in ECNU for five years and get the PhD in 2021. He finished postdoctoral scholar at Fudan NLP, the director is Prof. Xuanjing Huang.

Research

My research interests include

  • Natural Language Processing

  • Large Language Models

  • Continual Learning
